FAQs

How do I place an order with Indus Books?

Ordering from our website is simple, you have two options you can either setup an account with us or use Express Checkout. Order any item on our website by simply clicking the “Buy Now” button on the product page and your purchase process will begin!
We highly recommend that you setup an account with us so we stay in touch with you about our exclusive online offers. Setting up an account also enables you to do the following:

    Track your order online.
    Review your purchase history.
    Receive updates on special offers, events, and new products and services.
    Shop and check out faster on your next visit.
    Retain items in your cart for future purchases

How do I setup an account on Indus Books?

Setting up an account with us is easy! Click on the ‘Register’ button on the top menu. Fill out our registration form (we promise we won’t ask for this information again). Once registered, you are all set to browse and order on our website
Also, you also have the option of registering through your Facebook or Google accounts

How do I sign into my account?

Signing in is simple, click on the ‘Login’ button on the top menu. Enter your email address and password and you are good to go.

How do I edit the information in my account?

To edit your account information, you need to go to your account page by clicking on your name in the top bar. This will take you to your account profile and allow you to edit your account information.

What are my payment options?

Customer convenience is important to us. We offer the following payment options on our website:

    Cash on Delivery
    Visa/MasterCard
    Bank Draft / Pay Order
    UBL Net-Banking
    Easypaisa / Jazzcash
